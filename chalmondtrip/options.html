How to use this guide.

This is a menu, not a march. Each city has 3–8 sections (lodging, wagyu, clubs, markets, etc.) with multiple curated options at different price points. Some are budget anchors, some are splurges.

For each card: a description, why this pick, the price band per person (USD), Google rating, and a one-click link to Maps. Open map.html alongside this to see geography. Open planner.xlsx to slot picks into days and watch the total.

A sample itinerary using a working selection is in itinerary.html — built to come in around $100/day with two splurges baked in.
